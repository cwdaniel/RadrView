About

RadrView is a free, open-source weather radar viewer with a hybrid architecture: MRMS composite tiles at z2–z7 (1 km, ~2 min), and NEXRAD Level 2 direct station data at z8+ (250 m native resolution, ~5–10 min from archive). Select stations provide real-time sweep display via chunk data. Licensed under the MIT License.
